How To Play

Take Up The Sword

Each level shows you a verse to study. When the battle begins, its words fall from above — tap them in order, first to last, before the devil's grey chaff leads your blade astray. Miss a word or strike the wrong one and you'll lose a Shield of Faith; lose them all and the fight ends.

Easy — a gentle drill. The verse lingers on screen a few seconds after the fight starts before fading away.
Standard — the good fight. No on-screen hint once the battle begins — you strike from memory.
Valiant — quit you like men. No hint, and no gold tell marking the true words from the chaff — every word looks the same.
