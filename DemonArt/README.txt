SWORD OF THE LORD — demon sprite assets
=========================================

These are the individual demon images cropped out of the original
concept art sheet, resized to 340px wide and compressed as JPEGs —
this is exactly what's embedded (as base64) inside sword-of-the-lord.html.

Files: <demon>_<state>.jpg
States: "laughing" = idle/taunt pose, "struck" = hit reaction

Demons:
  flameimp        (Level tier 1)
  hellhound       (Level tier 1)
  wraith          (Level tier 2)
  boneguard       (Level tier 2)
  bladefiend      (Level tier 3)
  shadowdervish   (Level tier 3)
  satan           (Final boss)

Note on resolution:
  The 6 regular demons were cropped from a shared grid in the original
  sheet, so their native resolution is only ~149x280px — these JPEGs
  are upscaled slightly to 340px to look right at game size. Satan's
  two images came from the larger bottom band of the sheet and are
  much higher native resolution (~600x389px).

  If you want sharper enemy art later, regenerate the 6 regular demons
  individually at full resolution rather than cropping from a shared sheet.
